Recent Work

Project Brawl

First real project in future games made in 2 weeks: A 2D side-scroller 'Super smash bros' inspired party game were up to 4 players can take part in fighting some of our custom-made characters.

Made in: Unity C#.

Float

My second big project which should combine at least two different sports. Flot combines rafting with archery in party game setting, you and your teammates need to work toghter to destroy the enemies raft by throwing explosive barrels or by shooting of the crew and making them crash in to obstacles in the level.

Made in: Unreal C++ and blueprints.

Dayspring

Dayspring is an atmospheric stealth game set in a early 20th century Sweden. Play as a boy searching for his grandpa, whom suddenly had left their farm in the middle of the night. but during this venture into the night the boy has nothing but himself and his lantern.

Made in: Unreal C++ and blueprints.

Bad hare day

Bad Hare Day is a 2.5D puzzle side scroller where the player needs to manipulate gravity onthemselves, as well as other objects in the world, in order to progress. The multi-layered circular world of Bad Hare Day complements the gravity swapping mechanics, and enablesinteresting puzzles that otherwise wouldn’t be possible.

Made in:Unreal C++ and blueprints.
